About The Citizen Information Group

Founded in Ottawa, Canada – Citizen is a not for profit, civil technology enterpise, founded to create and advocate for civil change

The Citizen Information Group at its heart is focused on developing novel ways to increase access and use of social services. We believe through automation, technology, greater ease of access and awarness of services. We can reduce overhead and waste, and keep all resources focused on providing help thoes that need it most

Built to promote Canadian values, Citizen aims to be on the forefront of the Online to Offline shift, interactive urbanization (Smart Cities) and personalization/tailoring of data of services and governance

Our Current Projects

Occasional Transpo

Occasional Transport is a website that provides information about Ottawa’s LRT status, alerts and cancellations. It is an informative and satirical website built to communicate the status of the Ottawa Light Rail Transit (Ottawa LRT) and OC Transpo Bus lines.
