About The Citizen Information Group

Founded in Ottawa, Canada – Citizen is a not for profit, civil technology and information company, founded to promote civil interests, social services and change

The Citizen Information Group at its heart is focused on developing novel ways to increase access and use of social services. We belive though greater access to service, we can reduce waste and get people the help they need as they need it

Built to promote Canadian values, Citizen aims to be on the forefront of the Online to Offline shift, interactive urbanization (Smart Cities) and personalization/tailoring of data of services and governance

Our Current Projects

PotSnap

PotSnap is Facebook Messenger powered automated pothole reporting tool. It is very simple to use with only a few quick questions. After which, it anonymously reports your pothole to the City of Ottawa

PotSnap is faster then any other means and aims to be the tool of choice for Canada’s Pothole hunters
